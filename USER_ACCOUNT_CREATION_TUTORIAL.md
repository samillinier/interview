# How to Create Your Account - User Tutorial

## Welcome to the PRM Dashboard!

Thank you for your interest in joining the PRM Dashboard! We're excited to have you on board. This comprehensive guide will walk you through every step of creating your account, ensuring you have a smooth and secure onboarding experience.

## What is the PRM Dashboard?

The PRM Dashboard is your personal portal for managing your installer profile, accessing job opportunities, tracking your qualifications, and staying connected with the platform. Once you create your account, you'll have access to:

- **Profile Management**: View and update your personal information, qualifications, and certifications
- **Job Opportunities**: Browse and apply for available installation projects
- **Document Management**: Upload and manage important documents and attachments
- **Notifications**: Stay informed about important updates, job matches, and system messages
- **Account Security**: Secure access to your information with password protection

## Why Create an Account?

Creating an account gives you full control over your installer profile and enables you to:

✅ **Access Your Information**: View your interview results, qualifications, and profile status  
✅ **Apply for Jobs**: Browse available opportunities and submit applications directly  
✅ **Manage Documents**: Upload and organize your certifications, licenses, and important files  
✅ **Stay Updated**: Receive notifications about new job postings and important updates  
✅ **Track Your Progress**: Monitor your application status and profile completeness  
✅ **Secure Your Data**: Protect your information with a personalized password

## About the Account Creation Process

Our account creation process is designed with your security and convenience in mind. We use a **two-step verification system** that ensures:

🔒 **Email Verification**: We verify that you own the email address you provide, protecting you from unauthorized account creation  
🔒 **Secure Password Setup**: After email verification, you'll create a strong password that meets industry security standards  
🔒 **Automatic Username Generation**: No need to remember a separate username - we generate one from your email automatically  
🔒 **Quick and Easy**: The entire process takes just a few minutes to complete

## What to Expect

This tutorial will guide you through:

1. **Accessing the account creation page** - Multiple ways to get started
2. **Entering your email address** - Simple and straightforward
3. **Email verification** - A quick click in your inbox
4. **Creating your password** - Clear requirements and helpful tips
5. **Completing your account** - Final steps to get you logged in

**Estimated Time**: 5-10 minutes  
**Difficulty Level**: Easy - No technical knowledge required  
**Requirements**: A valid email address and internet connection

## Before You Begin

To ensure a smooth account creation experience, please have the following ready:

- ✅ **Your Interview Email Address**: **IMPORTANT** - You must use the exact same email address you provided during your interview. This is required so the system can verify and link your account to your interview records.
- ✅ **Email Access**: Ability to check your inbox (and spam folder if needed) for the verification email
- ✅ **Stable Internet Connection**: Reliable connection for completing the process
- ✅ **5-10 Minutes**: Set aside a few minutes to complete the process without interruption

## Ready to Get Started?

Great! Let's begin. The process is simple, secure, and designed to get you up and running quickly. Follow the steps below, and if you encounter any issues, refer to the troubleshooting section at the end of this guide.

## Important Notes

### Security Features:
- ✅ **Email Verification Required**: You cannot create a password without verifying your email first
- ✅ **Token Expiration**: Verification links expire after 24 hours for security
- ✅ **Auto-Generated Username**: Username is created automatically - no need to choose one
- ✅ **Strong Password Enforcement**: System requires strong passwords to protect your account
- ✅ **Email/Username Login**: Flexible login options for your convenience

### What Happens Behind the Scenes:
- Your email is verified before password creation
- Your password is securely hashed and stored
- A unique username is automatically generated from your email
- Your account is linked to your installer profile

### Troubleshooting:

**Problem: "Email not verified" error**
- **Solution**: Make sure you clicked the verification link in your email before creating a password

**Problem: "Account already exists" error**
- **Solution**: You may already have an account. Try logging in instead of creating a new account

**Problem: Verification link expired**
- **Solution**: Request a new verification email from the create account page

**Problem: Password doesn't meet requirements**
- **Solution**: Check the error message for specific requirements and adjust your password accordingly

**Problem: Can't find verification email**
- **Solution**: 
  - Check spam/junk folder
  - Wait a few minutes
  - Click "Resend Email" button
  - Verify you entered the correct email address

**Problem: Account not linking to interview or verification fails**
- **Solution**: Make sure you're using the exact same email address you provided during your interview. The system requires this email to match your interview records. If you used a different email during the interview, contact support for assistance.
